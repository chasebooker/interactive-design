Enlightenment Today?
home how

The Buddha achieved enlightenment after sitting beneath the Bodhi Tree for forty-nine days. Today, perhaps, there is no need for our physical bodies to sit.

I have created a digital surrogate who will sit in my place. Beginning on April 8th, 2015, the surrogate will begin meditating in a simple chair facing a plain white wall.

After forty-nine days, the surrogate will achieve non-attachment and I, being indistinguishable from my surrogate, will as well.

This project exists only on the Internet and thus has no fixed location. Being stored on spinning hard disks, the surrogate will exist with or without a viewer.

At the end of the forty-eight days, the surrogate will be removed from the server and cease to exist, allowing the storage space to return to its natural, undefined state.
